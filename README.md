# 📊 Amazon Sales Data Analysis Dashboard

## 🎯 Project Overview
This project involves a comprehensive analysis of Amazon sales data using Microsoft Excel. The primary objective of this project is to uncover actionable business insights regarding product demand, brand performance, and the impact of pricing strategies (discounts) on consumer purchasing behavior. 

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
* **Microsoft Excel:** Data Cleaning, Data Formatting, and Data Modeling.
* **Excel PivotTables:** Data aggregation and summarization.
* **Data Visualization:** Interactive Dashboards, Bar Charts, Donut Charts, and Custom Formatting.
* **Slicers:** Interactive filtering by Product Category and Sentiment.

## 💡 Key Business Insights
Based on the data analysis, here are the major findings:
1. **High Demand Products:** The highest volume of sales is driven by everyday tech accessories, specifically **Cables** and **In-Ear** headphones.
2. **Top Performing Brands:** **Samsung** and **OnePlus** lead the market in terms of total revenue generation.
3. **Discount Impact Strategy:** There is a strong correlation between discounts and demand. The data reveals that offering a **50% - 60% discount** is the "sweet spot" that drives the maximum product demand.
